# Noise pollution

## Effects on health
- Prolong exposure can cause
    - Cardiovascular disease
    - Stress and anxiety
    - Physical damage to inner epithelia cells of ear
    - Hearing loss

## Effects on nature
- Terrestrial animals
- Aquatic animals
    - Cetaceans are vulnerable to noise pollution from sonar beams
    - Sound travels faster and longer and in water
